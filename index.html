Health & Hygiene
We use hospital grade cleaning and disinfectant products for your safety. Vehicles are sanitized after every trip, using non-toxic eco-friendly disinfectants. We provide masks for all passengers and actively follow all CDC guidelines to preventing the spread of the COVID-19 virus.

Safety
Our drivers are professionally trained and experienced. Each staff member undergoes a rigorous review process before joining the team which includes federal background checks, prior work reference checks, and more. Apart from initial pre-hire drug and alcohol tests, our drivers undergo and pass random testing to ensure your continued confidence with us.

Vehicle/Fleet Quality
Our company is owned by a Logistics professional. We are fully insured and staffed by friendly, licensed, and experienced team members. Our well-maintained vehicles can handle up to two oversized bariatric wheelchairs with a sturdy, rear passenger entry and can comfortably accommodate travel companions. Our shuttle busses can accommodate 2 wheelchairs and 6 people comfortably.
